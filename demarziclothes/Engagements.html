A transparent production De Marzi creates few clothes, but clothes that have a history. It is possible to trace the history of the garment you are interested in, from the exact origin of its raw material, to its sale. A clean company In a logic of sustainable development, De Marzi is committed to produce in a clean way. No material or fabric is sourced from intensive agriculture, but only at the source, from small local producers. The brand avoids producing clothes that are too polluting, such as jeans, in favor of linen pants for example. Finally, these are clothes that last in time and timeless, which allows to cut with the tendency of the fast-fashion, of production and consumption to excess. A fair company De Marzi tries to be as much as possible with everyone, whether it is with the producers, the seamstresses, the assemblers, or even the consumers. The clothes are sold at a high price, but a price that is justified. The brand takes advantage of the know-how of different regions of the world to produce its clothes in small workshops.
Quality products De Marzi is committed to creating exceptional products using unusual fabrics, materials and techniques. You won't find such products at other designers. The garments are guaranteed for 5 years, if there is a problem, you can send it to our after-sales service free of charge and they will take care of the repairs. They do not move when washed, are wrinkle-free fabrics and dry quickly. Concerning the technical characteristics, each garment has its own particularity (thermo heating, elastic, anti-perspirant...).
