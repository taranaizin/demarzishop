Story of our clothes

The Amsterdam blouse

The Amsterdam blouse is made entirely of silk from Sichuan, a region of China specialized in silk production. The small Chinese producers are directly selected and paid by the company.

The silk is then taken to Amsterdam, along with other materials, to refill the tank from time to time and limit the use of transport, to limit the emissions that result from it.

In Amsterdam there is a workshop with 4 very talented seamstresses, specialists in silk confections, they know how to handle this material to perfection. The blouse is colored with natural saffron dye. The patterns used were designed by Livia De Marzi directly.

They have been elaborated to create a chic, but comfortable and above all light garment.

The characteristics of this garment are the flexibility, the comfort, but also the resistance of this blouse. It is wrinkle-free and cannot tear or hole. This is thanks to a very special weaving technique, whose secret we cannot reveal.

This blouse is rather loose, if you want to wear it more fitted, do not hesitate to take a size below.
